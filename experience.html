My experiences

Hi there, you are already familiar with who i am and what i do and some of my projects but i wanted to make a more personal chat about some of my experiences through life.
If you already dont know i am a gay man and this has affected my life quite alot and has completely altered my life so i wanted to share my experiences with you.
Although i have came to accept myself for who i am i know i will get judged by some people in real life for something i have no control over and i have experienced hate for being gay.

Even though these comments do affect me it still wont stop me from being who i am and i will stand againt the hate and help people in need who cant fight for themselves.
I do this because even though i may not know them they are still human and everyone deserves a voice to be heard and helped if they are struggling or need someone to reach out to.
everyone is human and should be treated as such even if they are different from the average person.
